Background
Education, story, and what drives the work

I'm Dhruv Bhavsar, a software engineer based in Ottawa. Over the past 2.5 years at the Ottawa Hospital Research Institute, I've built and deployed a complete clinical AI system — from GPU video pipelines and edge computing on NVIDIA Jetson hardware, to Flutter mobile apps, REST APIs, and clinician-facing web dashboards. That system is live at The Ottawa Hospital and CHEO.

Before Ottawa, I spent nearly two years at LTIMindtree in Mumbai as a full-stack Java developer — building enterprise features for American Honda Motors and Honda Canada, leading a team of 9, and owning deployments across DEV, UAT, and production. I've also completed co-ops at Nokia and the Canada Revenue Agency, where I worked on systems touching millions of users.

I hold an M.Eng in Electrical and Computer Engineering from the University of Ottawa and a B.Eng from the University of Mumbai (9.68/10 GPA). I care about well-structured code, measurable outcomes, and software that works reliably in the real world.

I've solved 600+ DSA problems on LeetCode — consistent practice that keeps my fundamentals sharp across arrays, graphs, dynamic programming, and system design.
